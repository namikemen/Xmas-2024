As I look back on this year

I realize that even though

this is our second year together

we’ve experienced so many firsts.

Each one has brought us closer

taught us more about each other

and filled our lives with memories that

I will cherish forever.

What makes me even more excited is knowing that

there are so many more “first times”

waiting for us in the future.

I can’t wait to see what they’ll be

where they’ll take us

and how they’ll help us grow together.

I want you to know that

I truly enjoy every single moment I spend with you

whether it’s a big milestone

or a simple quiet day.

You make everything better

and I promise to keep trying my best

no matter what life brings

so we can go hand in hand all the way to the end.

Thank you for being my partner, my love

and my best friend.

I can’t wait to see what beautiful moments

the future holds for us.

Happy Birthday once again, my love.

Here’s to many more adventures together.
